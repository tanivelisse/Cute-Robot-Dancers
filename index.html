Cute Robot Dancers

Test your robot moves!

INSTRUCTIONS:
To begin each player must pick their robot character by clicking on their images, starting with Player1, followed by Player2.
Player1: To make your robot dance press the key A for ⇦, W for ⇧, D for ⇨ and S for ⇩.
Player2: To make your robot dance press the key J for ⇦, I for ⇧, L for ⇨ and K for ⇩.
You must match your robot's movements with the arrows that appear at the bottom. Each match you make will raise your coolness meter, but be careful with mismatches because they bring your coolness down.
The first robot to get to 10 wins the dance-off!
